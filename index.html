Help Center

How do I read the outlook maps?

The maps show areas of potential windstorm activity. Colors represent different confidence or intensity levels. Refer to the glossary for term definitions.

Why can't I see the latest outlook?

Sometimes the site cache delays updates. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

Can I report weather damage?

No, but it may be a feature in the future.

How can I report bugs?

You can report bugs by reaching out to us on X - @StormTrackerEU.

Who maintains this site?

The EWPC project is run by independent weather enthusiasts, not an official meteorological organization.
